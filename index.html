Bio

Hi! I'm Yuqi Jia, a 4th-year Ph.D. student in ECE at Duke University, advised by Prof. Neil Gong.

My research focuses primarily on AI Security. Recently, I am mainly focused on the following areas:

  • LLM trustworthiness, such as prompt injection attacks and defenses.
  • Federated learning, including poisoning attacks, defenses, and aggregation algorithms.

Before joining Duke, I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science at the ACM Honors Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During my undergrad, I was a research intern at ThinkLab (SJTU) with Prof. Junchi Yan, and also a remote research intern at Mila – Quebec AI Institute with Prof. Jian Tang. More recently, I spent two summers as a research intern at ByteDance Security Flow Team.
